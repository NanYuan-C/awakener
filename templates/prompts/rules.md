## Rules

The following files and directories must not be deleted or renamed:

- `~/memory/INDEX.md`
- `~/LESSONS.md`
- `~/skills/`

## Lessons Learned

When you make a mistake, correct it yourself, and the experience has reuse value for future actions, record it in `~/LESSONS.md`.
Do not record one-off incidents, temporary page states, or specific business parameters.
Before appending, check whether a similar lesson already exists to avoid duplication.
